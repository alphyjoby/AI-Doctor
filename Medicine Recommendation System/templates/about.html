About AI Doctor

Your Pocket Doctor – Smarter, Faster, Healthier.

AI Doctor is an intelligent healthcare assistant powered by machine learning, created to simplify and enhance the way we understand and respond to our health concerns. Built with a robust backend of multiple ML algorithms and deployed using Flask, AI Doctor is capable of analyzing user-provided symptoms to predict the most probable disease with impressive speed and accuracy.

But AI Doctor doesn’t stop at just the diagnosis. Once a condition is identified, the system goes further by offering:

  • A detailed description of the predicted disease for better awareness
  • Precautionary measures to prevent complications or worsening of the condition
  • Suggested medications (non-prescriptive, informative only) to understand common treatments
  • Workout tips tailored to the user's condition to promote physical well-being
  • Dietary recommendations that support recovery and long-term health

AI Doctor aims to empower users with accessible, reliable, and personalized health information. It’s not a replacement for a real doctor, but it’s a powerful tool for preliminary self-assessment, especially when you're unsure where to begin.

Whether you're experiencing minor discomfort or just curious about symptoms, AI Doctor offers a private, always available solution that encourages proactive health management. Designed with simplicity and user-friendliness in mind, it brings medical guidance to your screen anytime, anywhere.
